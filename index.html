PLANET'S SMASH

HOW TO PLAY?

1. DEFEND THE COORDINATES
Enemies are targeting specific locations on Earth. Identify the correct location to intercept them.

2. CORRECT ANSWER = DEFENSE
Answer correctly to fire your Hero's beam and destroy the threat. Earth heals.

3. WRONG ANSWER = IMPACT
If you miss, the bomb hits the real-world location. Earth takes damage.

4. WATCH THE TIMER
You have limited time to lock on the target.

5. HERO ABILITIES
Choose your hero wisely. Each has unique visual effects and dialogue.
