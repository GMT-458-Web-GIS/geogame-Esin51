PLANET'S SMASH

HOW TO PLAY?

1. Enter your name
Type your player name on the main screen and press Continue.

2. Choose your hero
Pick one of the four heroes. Each has different power, speed and cooldown.

3. Destroy as many targets as possible
Smash the geo-targets on the map as fast and as accurately as you can.

4. Watch the timer
You only have 60 seconds. Every second matters.

5. Avoid penalties
Missing or hitting wrong locations will reduce your score.

6. Beat the high score
Try to set a new record and become the ultimate Planet Smasher.

GOOD LUCK!
