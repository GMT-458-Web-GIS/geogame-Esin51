PLANET'S SMASH

HOW TO PLAY?

1. Enter your name
Type your name on the main screen and press Continue.

2. Choose your special ability
Each character will have different power, speed and cooldown.

3. Destroy as many targets as possible
Smash the geo-targets fast and accurately.

4. Watch the timer
You only have 60 seconds. Every second counts.

5. Avoid penalties
Missing or hitting wrong spots reduces your score.

6. Beat the high score
Try to become the ultimate Planet Smasher.

GOOD LUCK!
